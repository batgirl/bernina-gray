 What is a Component Pack?

Just like the default components packs available for iPhone, Android, Web..., You can create your own pack and add custom components to it. Custom component packs are available across projects and also can be shared with other Team Members.



NOTE: Custom component categories are automatically shared for all Team Members with TeamPack or Enterprise licenses only.
